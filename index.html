Thomas Degueule

Research Scientist, CNRS.
Software engineering. Meta-stuff.

SE Group @ LaBRI

I am a CNRS researcher in the Software Engineering group of LaBRI (Bordeaux, France). Previously, I was for three years a post-doc researcher in the Software Analysis and Transformation group of CWI (Amsterdam, Netherlands). Before that, I was a pre-doc researcher in the DiverSE group of IRISA (Rennes, France).

My main research interests include:

  • Software evolution, libraries, APIs
  • Empirical software engineering
  • Software language engineering
  • Domain-specific languages
  • Exploratory and live programming
  • and software engineering as a whole!

More information in my CV.
